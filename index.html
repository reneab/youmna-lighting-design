about

What is light?

Light is a powerful medium.

It is intangible, invisible, ethereal and ever-changing, yet it can transform and reveal spaces, provide ambience, simulate textures, trigger emotions and influence behaviour.

As Architectural Lighting Designers, we use both light and darkness to enhance the spatial experience in the built environment. Despite common beliefs, we don't see light as a decorative add-on, but as a fundamental tool to reveal the architectural design.

How do we work with light?

We get involved at the first stages of the design process to carefully understand design briefs, client needs and budget constraints. Working closely with architects and designers, we develop narratives in which light defines the core character of the architectural design and create unique atmospheres.

Our independent take on existing technologies and luminaires manufacturers allows us to maintain an unbiased approach. Through the use of lighting software, samples testing and mock-ups, we carefully select luminaires by evaluating cost, quality and performance to successfully deliver our lighting schemes.

If you wish to know more about our services and process please contact us at youmna@yald.co.
