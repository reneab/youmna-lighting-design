about


Youmna is an award-winning Architectural Lighting Designer based between Beirut and London.

Youmna pursued a BA in Interior Architecture at the Lebanese Academy of Fine Arts in Beirut, and a MSc at Scuola Politecnica di Design in Milan.

Dealing with long hours of electricity shortages in Lebanon sparked Youmna's interest in light and darkness at a young age. After several years in the Lighting Design industry, in 2016 Youmna joined Speirs+Major, known as one of the world's leading Lighting Design practices based in London. While at Speirs+Major she worked on several notable projects including Expo 2020 and Observation Tower in Dubai, Mini Living in Shanghai, New Doha International Airport, Is Molas Resort in Sardinia, Fortnum & Mason, Hide Restaurant and Goldman Sach's Fitness Center in London.

Youmna's passion for light has lead her to win several awards including Young Lighter of the Year 2015, and both Best Creative Installation and Peer Prize with the Society of Light and Lighting for "A Walk Towards Rose" Lighting Installation during the Ready Steady Light 2016 Event in London.

In 2019 Youmna established her practice betwen Beirut and London, and is currently working on several projects in Northern Europe, Middle-East and South-East Asia.

philosophy


Light is a powerful medium.

It is intangible, invisible, ephemeral, yet it can transform and shape spaces, reveal architecture, provide ambiance, simulate textures, trigger emotions and influence behavior.

As an Architectural Lighting Design practice, we use both light and darkness to enhance the spatial experience and visual environment, through close collaborations with architects on projects of wide-ranging types and scales.

By carrying out thorough studies and carefully listening to architect briefs and client needs, our lighting schemes aim to reveal the essence of the architectural design, defining its core character and creating unique atmospheres.

We remain independant yet collaborate with various lighting manufacturers and suppliers worldwide. Through the use of lighting softwares,samples and mock-ups, we carefully select luminaires by evaluating cost, quality and performance to successfully deliver our lighting schemes.
